Polish Movie Posters

1947 - 1989

Genuine

Original Polish Masterpieces

Rare • Out-Of-Print • Mostly from the Socialist Time

Discover Photographs of the Original Movie Poster Collection - dating from 1947 to 1989. Created by well-known Polish Artists such as Andrzej Pagowski, Wieslaw Walkuski, Jakub Erol, Lech Majewski, Eugeniusz Skorwider and others. The movie posters have been crafted for the first screening of international and Polish movies in Polish cinemas.
